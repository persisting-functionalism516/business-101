## 🧭 What this is

business-101 is a set of study notes for business and finance. It brings many core topics into one place so you can read, review, and build a strong base step by step.

It covers:

- Accounting
- Business and management
- Economics
- Finance and investment
- Marketing and branding
- Entrepreneurship and funding
- Legal and tax basics
- Data analysis
- Supply chains and operations
- AI use in business
- Ethics, strategy, and leadership

The content is in Traditional Chinese and fits self-study, class review, and exam prep.

Each topic is written as a study note with:

## 📝 Best way to study

Use this order for the best flow:

1. Start with economics and accounting
2. Move to finance and money flow
3. Learn marketing and business strategy
4. Study operations, supply chain, and project work
5. Add law, ethics, and governance
6. Finish with investment, valuation, and corporate finance
7. Review AI, data analysis, and digital change

If you are new to business, begin with one topic per day.
